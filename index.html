Before You Buy

Start with one tool, not a set

A single adjustable dumbbell or kettlebell gets used far more often than a matching rack of fixed weights nobody has room for.

Floor protection isn't optional

A mat under weights protects the equipment and the floor beneath it — cheaper than replacing either.

Bodyweight-first still wins on value

A $20 pull-up bar unlocks more programming than most machines costing ten times as much.

Buy for the space you have, not the space you want

Fold-flat and stackable gear gets used; equipment that has to be worked around usually doesn't.
